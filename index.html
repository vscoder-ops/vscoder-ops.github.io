Plugin Reference

Chaos Plugin

Class system, durability mechanics & legendary armor

Core Rules

🚫
No Mending
Mending is disabled on all armor in combat
🚫
No Pearls
Ender pearls are disabled during fights
⚔️
Proficiency
Wearing armor you aren't proficient in reduces your damage output
🛡️
Enchant Standard
Protection IV on armor, Sharpness IV–V on weapons
🐴
Horse Bonus
A horse wearing your class armor type gets Speed III + Resistance
🛡️
Equal Protection
All class armor provides the same protection and durability as netherite

Durability System

YOUR DURABILITY RATING
0 — Class Lost 3+ Start 5+ Ability
Everyone starts at 3+ durability. The maximum is 5+.
Hitting 0 durability means you lose your class and must craft a class reroll item.

Higher level = slightly more durability  ·  Lower level = slightly less durability

Legendary Chain Armor

⛓️
Chain Legendary Set
Each piece grants a unique permanent bonus — mix and match freely
🪖 Helmet
Reduces all ability cooldowns by half.
🧥 Chestplate
Increases all durability values by 100.
👖 Leggings
Allows use of any class ability regardless of what armor you're wearing.
👢 Boots
Enemy class passives no longer affect you.

Crafting Ritual

🔮
Ritual Announcement
The moment you attempt to craft any class item or legendary piece, your coordinates are broadcast to every player on the server. A 30-minute countdown begins — survive until it finishes or lose the craft. Applies to all ritual events.
